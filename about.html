About AstroFact

Masters of Time Calculation

For over 15 years, ChronoCalc has been the trusted name in precise time calculations, serving clients worldwide with unmatched accuracy and professional expertise.

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by a group of passionate astronomers and ancient wisdom scholars, ChronoCalc emerged from the need to bridge traditional time calculation methods with modern precision technology.

What started as a small consultancy has grown into a global leader in time analysis, serving over 10,000 clients across 50 countries. Our unique approach combines time-honored astrological principles with state-of-the-art computational methods.

We believe that every moment holds significance, and our mission is to help individuals understand the precise timing that shapes their lives through accurate birth time analysis and astrological consultation.
